Introduction

Sofi is a versatile card game bot on Discord that allows users to collect and trade cards featuring a wide range of themes, including K-pop groups, video games, anime, manga, and more.

Designed to enhance the gaming experience within Discord servers, Sofi fosters community engagement and interaction among members who share similar interests. For example:

  • As a multi-server bot, Sofi can be integrated into various Discord communities, making its features accessible to all users in any server where it is installed. This flexibility not only enriches individual gaming experiences but also encourages collaboration and competition among players across different servers.
  • Sofi offers a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to collect, trade, and showcase cards, adding an exciting layer of interactivity to your Discord experience. Whether you are a casual fan or a dedicated collector, Sofi provides an engaging platform to explore and celebrate your favorite franchises while connecting with like-minded individuals. With its emphasis on community and shared interests, Sofi is more than just a game; it’s a way to build connections and share passions within the vibrant Discord ecosystem.

    • The dropper has drop priority meaning others who grab the same card get 100% blocked and has no chance of getting that card for a certain amount of time.

    • Each card available in the drop is assigned a unique numbered button(e.g, button 1, button 2, etc.) Players must press the correct button to select the desired card.

  3. Vote - Players can vote for Sofi every 12 hours to get a single wist which is a kind of currency that can be used within the game. Voting on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday will give two wists.
    • Players can utilize the command svote to get the voting links and the player's progression towards the required number of votes per month.
    • Voting is crucial in the Sofi game to earn rewards, specifically monthly chests. Players must accumulate a designated number of votes within the month to qualify for these rewards.

General Info

The following information serves as a foundational resource for players to enhance their understanding and gameplay experience. Players will acquire this knowledge through their engagement with the game mechanics and environments. This information is intended to provide context and insights that facilitate informed decision-making during gameplay.

sburn or sburn [card code]

The "Burning" process allows players to dispose of selected cards in exchange for in-game currency. This currency can subsequently be utilized for various purposes within the game. Players may initiate the Burning process on cards they have acquired by typing in sburn(or sb) or sburn(or sb) [cardcode], effectively converting them into valuable assets for further gameplay advancements.

Cards that are generated (or "dropped" are assigned a generation(gen value). Each gen card generated within the system will have its gen value assigned within the definite range. Lower gen values indicate higher values for the dropped cards.

slookup or slookup [card code]

Lookup gives the player information about the character on the card, its versions and event versions. Players type in slookup (or slu) to look up the information. The information lookup provides:

  1. Series and Name of character
  2. How many wishlists the character has
  3. How many 2D versions the character has
  4. How many event versions the character has
  5. An image showing every 2D version

Players can view every 2D version of the character by the "Version" button and every event version by the "Event" button.

There are various methods players can utilize to find character information, for example players can do character lookup, series lookup, force search, and selection(from force search).

swishlist

The Wishlist feature enables players to indicate specific characters they are seeking. This functionality not only allows users to track their desired characters but also be able to get valuable insight in the character demands. The biggest features of Wishlist are:

  • Players can create and manage a list of characters they wish to acquire.
  • The number of wishlists associated with a character serves as a quantitative measure of that character's popularity. This metric can influence the perceived value of the character card.
  • Characters with higher wishlist counts may be viewed as more valuable due to increased interest from the community.

Guild

Players can make a guild of Sofi players that they regularly play together with. Guilds consist of four primary roles: Founder, Guildmaster, Veteran, and Rookie. Each role comes with specific permissions, with the Founder having the highest level of authority, including full administrative capabilities. The Guildmaster with a second authority level, and Veteran/Rookie having no authority level. Promotion within these roles should be approached carefully.

Guilds begin at Level 1 and can earn experience points (XP) through various activities. Upgrading the guild requires XP and enables additional features. Member capacity can also be expanded over time, allowing for more players to join. Guilds participate in seasonal competitions, where they are ranked based on performance in raids. Each season consists of active gameplay periods followed by a grace period, during which rankings are not updated.Guilds can purchase and upgrade various boosts that enhance performance in quests and crafting. Each boost requires a specific amount of resources and offers unique benefits, contributing to overall guild efficiency.

This general structure allows guilds to operate effectively, fostering teamwork and enhancing the gaming experience for all members.

Players can find more information on all of these features by viewing the sofi guide.

Currency and Trading

Currency

Currencies play a critical role in the Sofi ecosystem, facilitating transactions and interactions across various mechanics and features.Understanding the different types of currencies is essential for effective utilization of the game’s systems. Currencies are required for crafting, guild management, item purchases, trading, and other in-game activities.

  1. Wists - The wists currency is the most widely used and centralized form of currency within the Sofi ecosystem. It serves as the primary medium of exchange for in-game transactions and is predominantly utilized for player-to-player trades. Obtained from Voting.
  2. Silvers - Silvers are the “common” currency of Sofi and are mostly used for cosmetics. Obtained from burning cards.
  3. Cubes - Cubes are used exclusively to get a 3D version of a character. Obtained from burning cards
  4. Elixirs - Elixir is a Guild Exclusive currency
  5. Shards - Shards are a currency related to Guild and Raid/PvP activities
  6. Gems - Gems are a premium currency that requires real money to purchase outside of trading for it from other players and the market.

Cosmetics

Frames

Frames can be used to add a specialized border around a card. They are a lesser upgrade than a 3D, but players have the option to choose their frames for their card, it is also an alternative if the players specific card does not have a 3D.

There are five types of frames players can aquire. They are, Official, Custom, Monthly, Limited, and Legacy frames. Players can lookup buyable frames by typing in sframeshop where two Official frames and six custom frames are rotated daily.

Morph

Morphs are customizable color filters applied to a card, allowing for a wide range of visual effects. These filters can subtly adjust hues, apply tints, or even invert colors, offering extensive possibilities for unique card appearances.

  • On 2D’s only the frame or the type color will be changed.
  • On 3D’s the entire base card will change.

Players can buy a random morph for 350 silvers using smorph (or sm). Players can also undo the morph for 100 silvers using sundomorph (or sum).

Backgrounds

Backgrounds are 3D-exclusive cosmetics that enhance the card by filling in its gaps. They can be accessed and viewed using their ID with the sbackgroundshop (or sbgs, sbgshop) command.

Players can buy any background excluding Event Exclusive Backgrounds(only can be purchased when an event happens) by typing the command sbuy( or sget) and apply the background by the command sbackground (or sbg, subg)

Glows

Glows are a premium cosmetic in Sofi that add a blurred glow effect around a selected card, excluding GIFs. To obtain a glow, users can either unlock one or purchase it through glow-selling services available in Sofi Cafe and other Sofi-playing servers. Once unlocked, a glow requires recharging for continued use. Each recharge costs 65 gems, allowing for infinite recharges thereafter.

Normal glows cost 100 gems to unlock with the command sunlockglow (or sug) and once aquired players can use a glow by the command sglow. Players can also rename and undo a glow by the commands sglowname and sundoglow, respectively. A Super Glow is created by merging two glows, resulting in a gradient effect and a significantly brighter appearance. You need 2 available glows to merge for the Super Glow, which can be done with the ssuperglow command. All this will cost around 1000 gems.

More details will be on the Official Sofi Guide website.
